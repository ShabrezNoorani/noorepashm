Our Story: From Kashmir to France

Noor-e-Pashm is born from a deep passion for the rich cultural heritage of Kashmir. As someone who grew up amidst its breathtaking landscapes and vibrant traditions, I've always been captivated by the exquisite craftsmanship of its artisans, especially the legendary Pashmina shawls.

After moving to France in 2021 and gaining valuable experience in business, I realized my dream: to bridge the gap between the skilled hands of Kashmiri artisans and the discerning tastes of the European market. Noor-e-Pashm is more than just a business; it's a mission to bring the authentic beauty and timeless elegance of Kashmiri handicrafts to your doorstep.

We believe in fair trade, sustainable practices, and celebrating the stories behind each handcrafted piece. While we prepare our first collection of exquisite shawls and unique handicrafts, we invite you to explore our blog, where we share insights into Kashmiri culture, art, and the journey of our upcoming products.
